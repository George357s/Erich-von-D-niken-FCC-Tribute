Erich Von Däniken

An Author Shrouded in Controversy

A GrandFather of The Ancient Astronaut Theory

Erich (full name Erich Anton Paul von Däniken) is a Swiss Author and one of the main people responsbile for the rise in popularity of the Ancient Astronaut Theory. Through bold claims and questionable findings, he will force you to rethink how you believe our ancestors may have lived.
